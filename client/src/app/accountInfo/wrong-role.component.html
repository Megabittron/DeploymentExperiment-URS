Apologies for the mistake. This website is undergraduate research itself by the student developers who made this... isn't the site all about helping correct students anyways!

If you are supposed to be a co-presenter:

Co-presenters are assigned to their abstract by the presenter that submitted the abstract when the presenter submitted a co-presenter's x500 email. Email small203@morris.umn.edu to be properly assigned.

If you are supposed to be an Advisor:

Advisors are assigned to an abstract by the Gmail address that a student submitter provided for their advisor(s). Email small203@morris.umn.edu to be properly assigned.

If you are supposed to be an Admin or Chair:

Fellow admins and chairs would be glad to invite you into the club. Email small203@morris.umn.edu to be properly assigned.
